About Our College

History & Overview

**Katiadi Government College** is a prominent public educational institution located in Katiadi, Kishoreganj District, Dhaka Division, Bangladesh. Established on **July 1, 1968**, by the then headmaster of Katiadi High School, Mozzaffor Uddin Ahmed, the college was initially named "Katiadi College." In **2019**, it was nationalized and subsequently renamed Katiadi Government College. The college is affiliated with the Dhaka Education Board for higher secondary education and the National University for undergraduate programs.

The college is recognized for its commitment to quality education and has a student enrollment of approximately **3,000**.

Key Information
  • Location & Campus: Situated in the heart of Katiadi Municipality, the college spans approximately 7.78 acres (3.15 hectares). The campus is well-equipped with facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Academic Programs:

    Higher Secondary (HSC): Science, Business Studies, and Humanities

    Undergraduate (Degree Pass): Various disciplines under the National University

  • Notable Alumni: One of the distinguished alumni is Nur Mohammad, a former Inspector General of Police (IGP) and a former Member of Parliament for Kishoreganj-2 constituency.
